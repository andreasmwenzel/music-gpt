# Pop-Punk

## Parking Lot Polaroids

### Style

Pop-punk, 178 BPM. Bright power chords, driving bass, punchy drums, nasal lead vocal, gang backups, verse to huge chorus, quick halftime bridge, polished energetic mix.

### Lyrics

Write lyrics for Parking Lot Polaroids: old friends, summer rain, promises that almost broke. Verse/chorus/bridge, conversational lines, memorable hook, no profanity, bittersweet hopeful close.
