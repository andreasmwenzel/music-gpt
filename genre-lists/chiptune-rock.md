### Style

Chiptune rock, 164 BPM. Square-wave arps, bitcrushed lead, palm-muted guitars, snappy acoustic drums, dual vocal shouts, stop-start pre-chorus, soaring chorus, playful aggression.

### Lyrics

Write lyrics for 8-Bit Heartline: arcade lights, missed calls, retrying love after mistakes. Verse/chorus/bridge, punchy phrasing, repeated hook, no nostalgia cliches, upbeat close.
