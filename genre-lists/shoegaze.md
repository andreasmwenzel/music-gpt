# Shoegaze

## Saltglass Reverie

### Style

Dreamy shoegaze, 128 BPM. Layered fuzz guitars, driving bass, motorik drums, breathy duet vocal, wall-of-sound chorus, brief clean bridge, end with swirling feedback tail.

### Lyrics

Write lyrics for Saltglass Reverie: ocean haze, blurred memories, holding on without closure. Verse/chorus/bridge, impressionistic images, repeated hook phrase, gentle but determined voice.
