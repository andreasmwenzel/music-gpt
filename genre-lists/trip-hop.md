# Trip Hop

## Velvet Static Letters

### Style

Smoky trip hop, 92 BPM. Dusty breakbeat, upright bass, Rhodes chords, vinyl crackle, intimate alto vocal, sparse verses and late chorus bloom, tape-saturated texture.

### Lyrics

Write lyrics for Velvet Static Letters: unsent messages, midnight kitchen light, quiet regret turning to resolve. Free-verse verses with short chorus line, intimate tone, no profanity.
