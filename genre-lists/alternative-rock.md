# Alternative Rock

## Fluorescent Shoulder

### Style

Anthemic alternative rock, 136 BPM. Crunchy rhythm guitars, melodic lead line, tight live drums, warm bass, earnest tenor vocal, quiet verse into explosive chorus, short bridge.

### Lyrics

Write lyrics for Fluorescent Shoulder (Alternative Rock): gritty night-shift hope, earnest tenor. Quiet reflective verses, explosive anthemic chorus, concrete images, PG tone, resilient ending.

## Glass Riot Summer

### Style

Alternative rock, 142 BPM. Jagged guitar stabs, rubbery bass, dry punchy drums, urgent alto vocal, tense verse to wide hook chorus, gang-tail outro, minimal reverb.

### Lyrics

Write lyrics for Glass Riot Summer (Alternative Rock): heatwave street unrest turning into unity. Urgent clipped verses, big hook chorus, vivid city details, defiant but hopeful tone.
