## Fluorescent Shoulder

### Style

Anthemic alternative rock, 136 BPM. Crunchy rhythm guitars, melodic lead line, tight live drums, warm bass, earnest tenor vocal, quiet verse into explosive chorus, short bridge.

### Lyrics

Write lyrics for Fluorescent Shoulder: late-shift city nights, carrying friends through burnout, finding small wins. Verse/chorus/bridge, concrete images, singable hook, PG tone.
